About the author

Khadijah Coakley

Front-end Dev

Khadijah Coakley is a serial-entrepreneur, designer and front-end developer. Her present work may be summarized as “designing viable businesses around value-creating ideas”. She is the co-founder of Wildfyre, a collective of talented entrepreneurs, idea engineers and venture capitalists. Her growing expertise in advising early-stage technology companies centers on research-driven concept validation, niche product development, and establishing a strategic framework for assessing viability.
